General Questions

How does the AI Resume Generator work?

Our AI Resume Generator uses advanced artificial intelligence to create professional, ATS-friendly resumes in minutes. You simply input your information, and our AI analyzes it to generate optimized content, format it professionally, and create a ready-to-download PDF.

Is my data secure with your service?

Yes, we take data security very seriously. All your information is encrypted and we do not share your personal data with third parties. We only use your information to generate your resume and provide our services to you.

What makes your resumes ATS-friendly?

Our resumes are designed to pass through Applicant Tracking Systems (ATS) by:

  • Using clean, standard formatting that ATS software can easily parse
  • Including industry-specific keywords relevant to your field
  • Organizing content with standard section headings that ATS systems recognize
  • Avoiding complex design elements like tables, columns, and graphics that can confuse ATS
